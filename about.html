About Me

Building award-winning mobile experiences and leading technical teams for 12+ years

Professional Background

From building award-winning mobile apps to leading technical strategy as a CTO, my 12+ year journey in software development has been driven by turning complex problems into elegant, user-friendly solutions.

I started at Twidl Inc developing Epic Life, a Samsung Galaxy Gear game that won the Globe Samsung Regional App Challenge and represented the Philippines internationally. That early success taught me that great apps aren't just about code; they're about creating experiences that resonate with users.

I've contributed to high-impact projects including Kumu (Philippines' leading live streaming platform), Wandermaps (GPS-enabled interactive travel maps), and applications across healthcare, smart home technology, agriculture, and fintech sectors.

Core expertise: Android (Java, Kotlin), Flutter, backend architecture, MVVM/Clean Architecture, technical leadership, and full product lifecycle development.

I'm passionate about writing clean maintainable code and building products that make a real difference. Let's connect if you're interested in mobile development, technical leadership, or building great software.
